So, how does it work?

First, include Impress.js into your html file. <html> ... ... <body> ... ... <script type="text/javascript" src="impress.js"></script> <script>impress().init();</script> </body> ... ... </html>
The basic structure - impress and step <div id="impress"> <div class="step"> Slide1 </div> <div class="step"> Slide2 </div> <div class="step"> Slide3 </div> </div>
, but this is not all...
It's the coordinate that matters! <div id="impress"> <div class="step" data-X="0" data-y="0"> Slide1 </div> <div class="step" data-X="1000" data-y="0"> Slide2 </div> <div class="step" data-X="2000" data-y="0"> Slide3 </div> </div>
Don't forget there's z axis! <div class="step" data-X="5000" data-y="0" data-z="0"> And don't forget there's <b>z</b> axis! </div>
推 <div class="step" data-X="5000" data-y="0" data-z="1500"> 推 </div>
推推 <div class="step" data-X="5000" data-y="0" data-z="3000"> 推推 </div> OK, enough with 推推, what else can we do?
We can do 轉 <div class="step" data-X="..." data-y="..." data-z="..." data-rotate-y="45"> We can do <b>轉</b> </div>
轉轉 <div class="step" data-X="..." data-y="..." data-z="..." data-rotate-y="90"> 轉轉 </div>
